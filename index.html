CAPABILITIES

Advanced Solutions

01

SLM for Stainless Steel

Selective Laser Melting for 316L and 17-4 PH stainless. Full melt pool fusion achieves 99.9% density with superior corrosion resistance and biocompatibility for medical, marine, and food-grade applications.

02

Tool Steel & Maraging

H13 hot-work tool steel and M300 maraging via DMLS. Maraging 300 reaches 1965 MPa UTS after aging — ideal for injection molds, dies, aerospace fittings, and high-cycle tooling.

03

DED for Large Structure

Wire arc directed energy deposition for large-format steel structures and repairs. Build rates up to 4 kg/hr for structural steel components where powder-bed economics don't apply.

CAPABILITIES

Steel Alloy Expertise

Steel 3D printing demands precise process control per alloy — we optimize laser power, scan strategy, and post-processing for each grade.

  • ● 316L: 565 MPa UTS, excellent corrosion resistance
  • ● 17-4 PH: 1310 MPa in H900 condition
  • ● Maraging 300: 1965 MPa after aging — highest-strength steel option
  • ● H13 tool steel: hot-work die and mold applications
  • ● Full annealing, stress relief, and tempering available
  • ● CT scanning and CMM inspection on every production run
